03 — Our story

Built from a simple wish:
never lose a book again.

My Library Corner began with a familiar feeling—a shelf full of meaningful books, and no beautiful way to remember the life lived between their pages.

We wanted something warmer than a spreadsheet and quieter than a social network. A private place where a reader could keep the books they love, remember where they stopped, and watch a personal library become a portrait of who they are.

So we made this corner. It is deliberately simple, deeply personal, and designed to grow one carefully chosen book at a time.
